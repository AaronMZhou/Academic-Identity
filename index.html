Aaron Zhou's Academic Identity Website

This is my assignment for answering key questions about Ohio State University which I go to and I hope to get from it. I hope you will enjoy this small website! I wrote this website to have fun showcasing how I can use my major (CSE) and to answer this assignment in a unique way!

Why I Chose OSU

I chose Ohio State University because of its highly regarded Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) program and extensive experiential learning opportunities. OSU’s research in artificial intelligence and data science aligns with my passion for building impactful, innovative solutions. Additionally, OSU’s diverse community and collaborative environment attracted me. The mix of cultures and ideas here is ideal for fostering creativity and personal growth, which are essential to me.

What I Hope to Gain from My Experience

I hope to gain both technical expertise in programming and algorithms and essential soft skills like leadership and communication. My goal is to become a well-rounded engineer, and I plan to explore interdisciplinary programs in entrepreneurship and innovation to learn how to turn ideas into practical applications. The chance to participate in research initiatives, internships, and industry partnerships is particularly exciting, as it would allow me to apply what I’m learning and engage with professionals in the field.

What I Hope My Life Will Look Like in 10 Years

In ten years, I envision myself as a software engineer working on solutions that impact society, possibly in the fields of environmental sustainability or healthcare technology. I hope to be leading development teams, tackling meaningful projects, and continuously advancing my technical skills. I would also love to stay involved with OSU, whether by mentoring students, collaborating on research, or contributing to community service initiatives.

How OSU Can Help Prepare Me for the Life I Intend

OSU’s challenging coursework in data structures, artificial intelligence, and machine learning will build my problem-solving skills and prepare me for real-world challenges. Collaborative group projects will teach me how to work effectively with others, which is vital in a team-driven industry. Through co-op programs, career fairs, and the university’s network of alumni, I’ll gain practical experience, build valuable connections, and learn from accomplished engineers.

How I Will Cope with Disappointment, Discouragement, or Failure

I know that setbacks are a natural part of the learning process. When I face disappointment, I’ll take time to reflect on what happened and seek feedback from professors, mentors, or peers. I’m committed to using each experience as an opportunity for growth. By maintaining a growth mindset and staying focused on my long-term goals, I’m confident I can navigate any challenges that arise.
